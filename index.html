How LSAC GPA is Calculated?

Unlike standard university GPAs, the LSAC (Law School Admission Council) assigns a value of 4.33 to an A+. All failing grades (F) are weighted as 0.00, regardless of school policy. Our tool uses the standard 4.33 conversion table to ensure accuracy for your CAS report.
