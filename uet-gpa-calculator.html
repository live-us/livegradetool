How UET GPA is Calculated

The GPA is calculated using the formula:

GPA = Σ(Course Credit × Grade Point) / Σ(Course Credit)

Each course's marks are converted to grade points based on the official UET grading scale.

UET Grading Scale

Marks Grade Grade Point
