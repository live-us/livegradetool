About Us - Live Grade

LiveGrade.site is an educational platform dedicated to helping students calculate and understand their academic performance. Our primary tool allows users to calculate their GPA accurately, including specialized systems like the LSAC 4.33 scale used for law school admissions.

Our mission is to provide simple, accurate, and user-friendly tools that empower students to make informed academic decisions. We believe that understanding your grades and GPA is a crucial part of planning for higher education and career success.

Our Mission

At LiveGrade.site, our mission is to make academic performance transparent and easy to understand for students worldwide. We aim to provide accurate GPA calculations, practical insights, and educational resources that help students plan their academic journey effectively.

Our Values

  • Accuracy: Ensuring that all calculations and tools provide reliable results.
  • Clarity: Making complex grading systems easy to understand.
  • Accessibility: Providing free, user-friendly tools accessible to all students.
  • Integrity: Maintaining honesty, transparency, and trustworthiness in all our content.
  • Continuous Improvement: Constantly updating our tools and resources to meet student needs.

Who We Serve

LiveGrade.site is designed for students, educators, and academic professionals who want a clear understanding of academic performance and GPA calculations. Our users include:

  • High school students planning for college or law school.
  • College students calculating cumulative GPA or planning course loads.
  • Law school applicants navigating the LSAC GPA system.
  • Educators and counselors seeking accurate tools for academic advising.

Our Tools and Services

LiveGrade.site provides a range of tools designed to support students in understanding their performance:

  • GPA Calculator: Accurately calculates GPA on standard 4.0 and LSAC 4.33 scales.
  • Course Analysis: Helps students see how individual grades affect overall GPA.
  • Educational Resources: Guides, tips, and explanations to help students plan their academic journey.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Interactive, easy-to-use tools that require no account or login.

Our Commitment to Accuracy

At LiveGrade.site, accuracy is our top priority. All calculations provided by our tools are based on official grading scales and formulas, including the LSAC 4.33 system used for law school applications.

While we strive to provide precise results, users are encouraged to verify their GPA calculations with official academic records from their respective institutions.

Our Commitment to Users

Founded by Usama Saeed, LiveGrade.site is dedicated to creating a user-friendly and accessible platform. We ensure our tools require no login or personal information, maintaining a privacy-first approach to education.

Our website is designed to be fully responsive, making it easy for students to calculate their GPA on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices anytime, anywhere.
